Kanch Mandir

Kanch Mandir is a famous Jain temple in Indore known for its unique glass and mirror work. The temple was built by Sir Seth Hukumchand Jain and is dedicated to Jain Tirthankaras.

The walls, ceilings, and floors are decorated with intricate glass designs, making it an architectural marvel. Kanch Mandir is not only a place of worship but also a major tourist attraction.
