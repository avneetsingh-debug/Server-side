Rajwada Palace

Rajwada Palace is one of the most important historical monuments of Indore and a symbol of the city’s royal heritage. It was built by the Holkar dynasty in the 18th century and is located in the heart of the old city.

The palace shows a beautiful mix of Maratha, Mughal, and European architectural styles. Over the years, Rajwada has witnessed many historical events and restorations. Today, it attracts tourists for its grand structure, historical importance, and cultural value.
