Lal Bagh Palace

Lal Bagh Palace is a magnificent palace built by Maharaja Shivaji Rao Holkar and is one of the finest examples of royal architecture in Indore. The palace reflects European architectural influence in its design and interiors.

It once served as a residence for the Holkar rulers and hosted many royal ceremonies. The palace is surrounded by a well-maintained garden and now serves as a museum showcasing the luxurious lifestyle of the Holkar dynasty.
